Command Syntax

  • rice — Search/filter inventory
  • rice -1c — Deduct 1 cup
  • rice +2lb — Restock 2 lbs
  • +chicken 3lb — Add new (auto-detects category, 20% low stock alert)
  • +chicken 3lb @fridge #protein — Add with location/category
  • +chicken 3lb !0.5 — Add with custom low stock threshold
  • view rice — View item details
  • add or add paprika — Open Add form
  • recipe — Open Recipe section
  • scan or capture — Scan label/receipt (OCR)
  • help — Toggle this help panel
Unit Conversions
  • 1/4c oil — Quick convert (click target unit)
  • convert 1/4c oil to g — Volume to weight
  • convert 500g flour to cups — Weight to volume
  • convert 2T to ml — Simple volume conversion
  • convert — Open conversion reference
Inventory Actions
  • sort — Sort by urgency (low stock + expiring first)
  • sort name — Sort alphabetically
  • sort exp — Sort by expiration
  • sort qty — Sort by quantity
  • delete rice — Delete item (Enter twice to confirm)
  • edit rice — Edit item
  • chart salt or graph salt or price salt — View price history chart
  • set rice 5 — Set quantity to 5
  • staple rice — Toggle staple status
Move Between Locations
  • move chicken 5lbs to freezer — Move to freezer (auto-detect source)
  • move chicken 2lbs freezer fridge — Move from freezer to fridge
  • chicken >2lbs fridge — Shorthand move to fridge

Expiration auto-updates: Freezer items last ~36x longer. Defrosting gives 3 days for protein.

Filters
  • @fridge — Show fridge items only
  • #protein — Show protein items only
  • low — Show low stock items
  • expiring — Show expiring items
  • clear — Clear all filters

Units: t=tsp, T=tbsp, c=cups, oz, lb, g, kg, ml, l

Locations: @fridge, @freezer, @pantry, @spice, @counter

Categories: #protein, #dairy, #produce, #spice, #grain, #frozen, #baking
